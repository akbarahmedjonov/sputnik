Road to Fluency

Congratulations! You've completed all missions. Here's your roadmap to continued learning.

Comprehensive Courses & Grammar

  • Russian Enthusiast — A free, extensive online course from beginner to intermediate (B1). Features detailed grammar guides, vocabulary lists, and cultural sections.
  • Master Russian — Long-standing resource with clear lessons on the 6 Russian cases, verb conjugations, and common phrases.
  • The New Penguin Russian Course — Widely considered the best self-study guide for mastering Russian grammar and building a foundation for reading literature.

Listening & Immersion

  • Russian with Max — "Comprehensible input" content for intermediate learners. Podcast and YouTube covering real-life topics at a slower pace.
  • Slow Russian Podcast — Hosted by Daria Molchanova. Clear narration with vocabulary sections to understand culture and daily life.

Reading & Reference

  • OpenRussian — Essential dictionary and grammar database. Highlights word stress with full declension and conjugation tables.
  • Russian For Free - Texts with Audio — Short stories categorized by difficulty (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced) with translations and audio.

Practice Tools

  • Anki — Spaced repetition flashcards. Find pre-made "Top 2,000 Russian Words" decks to expand your vocabulary.
